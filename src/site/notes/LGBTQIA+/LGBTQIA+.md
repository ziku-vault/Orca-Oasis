Typically, the terms LGBTQ+ and LGBTQIA+ are widely used to reference the community of queer people. The acronym stands for lesbian (L), gay (G), bisexual (B), transgender (T), queer (Q), intersex (I), and asexual (A). Throughout many centuries, several slurs were reclaimed and used as an empowerment for the LGBTQIA+ community.
